Lineage 2 - Superion
https://l2wiki.com/main/updates/2919.html

## Class Changes
- Changes have been made to the base Critical Rate of various physical skills for Tyrr Warriors, Othell Rogues and Sigel Knights.
- Iss Enchanters have received changes to their shared attack skills, passive skills and buff and support skills, including new skills and modified effects.
- The base Critical Rate of some Iss Enchanter skills (Crippling Attack, Crushing Assault, Chaos Symphony) has been increased.
- Information about required weapons has been added to the descriptions of some Iss Enchanter skills.
- Iss Enchanter buff skills now take 3 slots instead of 5.
- Changes have been made to various Iss Enchanter skills, including Musician's Melody, Sonata Performance, Dance of Master, Song of Triumph, Drum Concert, Fantasia Harmony, Fantasia Melody and United Resistance.
- Some skills have been renamed, had their max levels increased or decreased and had their effects changed or removed.
- The Iss Dominator skills have been modified, including Fever Frenzy, Harmonic Frenzy and Serenity Frenzy, which provide various buffs to the player and their clan members.
- The Iss Hierophant skills have been modified, including Superior Divine Lore, Great Natural Superiority and Hierophant's Glory, which provide increased power and resistance.
- The Iss Sword Muse skills have been modified, including Superior Divine Lore, Grand Melody of Spirit and Sword Muse's Glory, which provide increased power and resistance.
- The Iss Spectral Dancer skills have been modified, including Superior Divine Lore, Grand Dance of Spectral Swords and Spectral Dancer's Glory, which provide increased power and resistance.
- The Iss Doomcryer skills have been modified, including Superior Divine Lore, Grand Soul Cry and Doomcryer's Glory, which provide increased power and resistance.
- The Iss Dominator's skills have been modified, including Dominator's Glory, Pa'agrio's Fist, Victories of Pa'agrio and Vanguard Valor.
- A new skill called Vanguard Will has been added, providing P. Skill Power, P. Skill Critical Rate and P. Skill Critical Damage buffs.

## Aeore Healer Changes
- Aeore Healer skills have been modified, including Dark Blast, Mass Dark Veil, Mark of Lumi, Dark Force and Dark Rain.
- New skills have been added to Aeore Healer, including Light Conviction and Saintly Patience.
- Some Aeore Healer skills have been removed, including Lakcis Disc, Sustain, Radiant Recharge and Summon Lumi.
- A new skill called Forgotten Power - Giant's Greed has been added, providing Attribute Attack and Resistance buffs.

## Hunting Zone and Boss Modifications
- The document section discusses various changes to the game, including skill changes, hunting zone reorganization and open world boss modifications.
- The reorganized hunting zones are divided into solo and party zones, with changes to monster numbers, respawn times and rewards.
- New open world bosses have been added, with non-golem bosses respawning every 5 hours and golems respawning daily at set times.
- Changes to loot drops for Lv. 116+ monsters in solo hunting zones have been made, replacing Aden's Treasure Chest with Aden's Shining Treasure Chest (Lv. 1).
- New field raid bosses have been added, including Demonic Venom, Avenger Graff, Mad Cullan, Chaos Golem #17, Chaos Golem #18 and Arrogant Lebruum.
- Rewards for defeating non-golem field raid bosses include contribution rewards, first hit rewards, last blow rewards and most damage dealt rewards.

## New Instances and Zones
- A new party instance zone called Empress' Palace has been added, with entry level set at Lv. 115+ and a weekly reset.
- Defeating Empress Ramona in Empress' Palace rewards players with items such as Magic Sphere Imbued with Chaos Power and Superion War Fortress's Time Stone.
- A new session zone called Superion War Fortress has been added, with entry level set at Lv. 115+ and a weekly reset.

## New Items and Equipment
- New L-grade orbs have been added, including Orb of Fire, Orb of Water, Orb of Air and Orb of Earth, which can be equipped in jewel slots and crafted using Wisdom Crystals.
- The Orb of Elements can be equipped in the brooch slot and provides various effects such as damage +10%, acquired XP/ SP +30% and received damage -10% for 30 days.
- A new gem called Amber has been added, which can be obtained by using a Mysterious Stone purchased with Chaos Aether and can be enchanted with Scroll: Enchant Amber.
- A new L-grade bracelet called Silvery Seed Bracelet has been added, which can be obtained by upgrading a +10 Superior Seed Bracelet Lv. 4 and provides various effects such as all stats +1, Attribute Attack +50 and Max HP/ MP/ CP +30%.

## Buffs and Cocktails
- Cocktail items have been replaced with new ones and their effects have been modified, including the addition of new buffs such as Fever Melody, Drumbeat of Serenity and Fantasia Harmony.
- Buffs obtained through Adventurers' Guide, Mentor, at the Olympiad and Ceremony of Chaos have been modified, including the addition of new effects such as Critical Damage for basic attacks +100% and Skill Cooldown -20%.
- New buffs have been added for Mentor, Olympiad and Ceremony of Chaos, providing various effects such as increased HP/MP recovery rate, vampiric rage and skill cooldown reduction.

## Collections and Item Effects
- New collections have been added in the categories of "Weapon", "Armour", "Accessories" and "Additional Supplies", offering various effects such as increased attack power, defense and resistance.
- Changes have been made to the Adventurers' Guide, including increased level requirements for buffs and the ability to purchase Special Cocktail.
- Visual effects have been added for Time Stones and the effects of R-grade fist weapons have been improved.
- The text describes various collections and items in Lineage II, including equipment, accessories and artifacts.
- These collections provide different effects, such as increased skill power, critical rate and resistance to debuffs.
- Some collections require specific items, while others have replacement items that can be used.
- The collections are categorized into "Special", "World" and "Field Raid" categories, each with unique effects and requirements.
- New items and collections have been added, including Mad Cullan's Ring, Arrogant Lebruum's Wing and Chaos Golem Cores, which provide various bonuses such as increased critical rate and damage.

## Gameplay Changes
- The level difference penalty between characters and monsters has been removed, but the penalty between party/clan members remains in effect.
- New quests have been added, including side quests related to field bosses and a weekly quest to defeat Chaos Golems.
- Crafting categories have been reorganized and new items have been added, including Masia's Soul Crystal, Pa'agrio's Soul Crystal and Eva's Soul Crystal.
- The mentorship system has been modified, with new items available for purchase with Mentee's Marks, including Vitality Sustaining Rune and Orb of Abyss.
- The relics summoning system has been modified and a new mechanic allows for guaranteed compounding of relics with 100% chance after accumulating 20 guaranteed compounding points.
- Relic compounding now displays the grade of the relic that can be obtained for B-grade and C-grade relics.
- The dye system has been changed, allowing tattoos to be upgraded up to Lv. 30 with Dye Powder and granting the Giant's Seal buff.

## System and Store Updates
- Castle Siege changes include reduced casting time for Seal of Darkness/Light, increased Siege Camp HP and P./M. Def. and new system messages.
- Einhasad's Store has added new items, increased the available amount of Ancient Beora Energy and removed certain items from sale.
- The Conquest store has modified some items, adding new ones and deleting others.

## Bug Fixes and Miscellaneous Changes
- Various other changes and bug fixes have been made, including changes to the payment confirmation letter, warehouse and party invitation system.
- Tales of Hero is now available on the world server.
- Fixed multiple issues with character appearances, skills and animations, including those related to Kamael, Dwarf and Shine Maker characters.
- Resolved various bugs and issues with items, quests, inventory and UI, including problems with auto-use, item reuse and incorrect item displays.
- Modified the use conditions for certain supplies, including Freya's Ice Rose and Glowing Dragon's Attribute Potion.
- Fixed crashes caused by specific chat commands and gamepad functions.


Lineage 2 - Shield of Kingdom
https://eu.4game.com/patchnotes/lineage2/453/

## Sigel Knight Rework
- The Sigel Knight class has been reworked, with enhanced combat abilities, new skills and changes to existing skills.
- Sigel Knight skills learned before Awakening (Novice skills) have also been changed.
- Enchant effects for various Sigel Knight skills have been changed or added.
- Certain Sigel Knight buffs can now be used together with Iss Enchanter's Angel's Touch and Aeore Healer's Brilliant Purge.
- Debuff Resistance values for some Sigel Knight skills have been changed.

## Sigel Subclass Skills
- Sigel Hell Knight skills include Black Thorn Chain Strike, Shadow Hunt, Dark Thorn of Revenge, Summon Panther Cubic, Avenger's Frenzy, Grand Thorn of Vengeance, Panther Rush, Avenger's Aura - Faith, Avenger's Bitter Cry and Hell Knight's Glory.
- Sigel Phoenix Knights skills include Wings of Fire (novel series), Phoenix Glide, Summon Phoenix Cubic, Flame Feather, Paladin's Frenzy, The Guardian, Paladin's Aura - Faith, Paladin's Fervent Cry and Phoenix Knight's Glory.
- Sigel Shillien Templar skills include Chained Death Scythe, Mass Lightning Strike, Summon Death Cubic, Summon Hex Cubic, Inhale Soul, Templar's Frenzy, Shillien's Grand Shadow, Templar's Aura - Faith, Templar's Spirited Cry and Shillien Templar's Glory.
- Sigel Eva's Templar skills include Gigantic Wave, Reversal Stream, Summon Othellot Cubic, Summon Spirit Hawk Cubic, Maelstrom, Guardian's Frenzy and other skills that provide various effects such as increased damage, defense and healing.
- Eva's Templar skills have been updated, including Grand Othellot's Wave, Guardian's Aura and Guardian's Thunderous Cry.

## Skill Changes and Fixes
- Sigel Death Knight skills have been changed, including Mocking Resilience and Valakas' Protection.
- Some skills have been modified to restrict resurrection/movement and other skills have been fixed to match their described effects.

## New Accessories
- New L-grade accessories have been added, including Silvery Brooch, Silvery Bracelet and Silvery Artifact Book.

## Zone Modifications
- The Tower of Insolence world session zone has been modified, with changes to terrain, monster placement and loot.
- The Swamp of Screams open hunting zone has been modified, with local monsters now dropping Stakato Tooth and Stakato Wing Collection item.
- A new open zone boss, Mutant Stakato Crog, has been added to the Swamp of Screams, spawning at 13:00, 16:00 and 20:00 daily.
- Rewards for defeating Mutant Stakato Crog include various items, such as Stakato Cocoon, Stakato Tooth and Stakato Wing, depending on the player's contribution to the battle.
- Several open zone bosses, including Ramona, Mimir, Isabella, Gigantic Golem, Spicula and Negative Garden Warrior, have been removed.

## World Olympiad Changes
- The World Olympiad timing has been changed to every Friday and Saturday from 21:00 to 22:40 and the level requirement for participation has been increased to 110+.
- The win conditions for the World Olympiad have been changed to require the opponent's HP to reach 0 and the title of Olympiad Hero or Legend now requires a certain number of victories.
- The alchemy system has been removed from the game and related items and resources will be refunded to characters' private warehouses.
- Characters who have not reached Lv. 110 can check Olympiad rewards and buy items from the Olympiad Manager using Marks of Battle.

## Relics and Collections
- New relics have been added, with some obtainable through the Radiant Relic Summon Coupon and others through compounding or replacing existing relics.
- New relic collections have been added, including Heir's Honor, Heir's Glory, Heir's Dignity and others, each with unique effects.
- New collections have been added, including Swamp of Screams - Tribute to the Golden Ram Army, Swamp of Screams - Preface to the Counterattack and others, each with unique effects and required materials.

## Einhasad's Store Update
- Einhasad's Store has updated its sale items, with Relic Summon Coupons and Shining Relic Summon Coupons available until October 30, 2024.

## UI and Other Changes
- Various UI changes have been made, including modifications to the mailbox, character status window and combat stats info.
- Other changes include fixes to issues with item exchange, quest windows and character animations, as well as updates to the game's mechanics and zones.
- Characters who choose Wynn Summoner as their dual class now receive a set of robes.
- Several issues with item enhancements, homunculi and skills have been fixed, including incorrect probabilities and effects.
- Various UI improvements have been made, including changes to button colors, item lists and skill descriptions.

## Game Update Introduction
- The Shield of the Kingdom update is scheduled for release on August 7, 2024.
- Skills that increased P. Skill Critical Rate based on the DEX value have been changed for Othell Rogues and Yul Archers.


Lineage 2 - Path of Rogue
https://eu.4game.com/patchnotes/lineage2/447/

## Othell Rogue and Adventurer Skills Update
- The "Path of a Rogue" update includes changes to Othell Rogue and Othell Adventurer skills.
- New effects and skills have been added, including the "Perfection" effect, which activates with a certain chance when attacking vital points and deals additional damage or triggers additional effects.
- Various Othell Rogue skills have been modified or removed, including changes to vital point attack mechanics, skill effects and visual effects.
- Othell Wind Rider skills have been modified or removed, with a focus on the Perfection effect, which allows for effective chaining of skills that attack the enemy's vital points.
- Othell Ghost Hunter skills have been modified or removed, with skills now delivering powerful blows after changing position relative to the enemy before attacking the target's vital point.
- Othell Fortune Seeker skills have been changed or removed, with the Perfect effect making the class stronger, particularly in PvE damage and Perfection trigger rate.
- Fortune Hunter skills have been updated, including Flip Lucky Coin, Fortune Seeker's Intuition and Coin Strike.

## Other Class Skill Changes
- Class skill changes have been made for Feoh Wizard, Shine Maker, Wynn Summoner, Sigel Death Knight, Yul Archer, Tyrr Warrior and Iss Enchanter.

## Anomaly Rebalance
- Anomalies have been rebalanced, with changes to debuff probability, resistance and new anomaly types, including Debilitation.
- Some skills and items have been rebalanced according to the new anomaly formula, including Angel's Touch and Brilliant Purge.
- Anomaly Resistance options have been modified in some hunting zones, including Neutral Zone and Tower of Insolence.

## Skill Changes and Fixes
- Skill Changes: Insolence and Conquest Debuff Resistance skills have been updated and issues with skill animations, descriptions and Debuff Resistance stats have been fixed.

## New Item: Silvery Circlet
- New Item: Silvery Circlet has been added, which can be obtained by upgrading +10 Radiant Warrior's, Wizard's, or Knight's Circlet and provides various stat bonuses.

## Hunting Zone Updates
- Hunting Zones: A new command channel instance zone, Spirit Seal, has been added and the Valakas raid has been modified, including changes to the boss's spawn cycle and loot privileges.

## Boss Modifications
- Boss Changes: World bosses Eigis, Bathin, Baium, Galaxia and Angel have been modified, including changes to their stats and XP rewards.

## Zone Modifications and Fixes
- Zone Changes: The layout of the Tower of Insolence has been modified and issues with instance zones and totems have been fixed.

## New Quests
- New Quests: New quests have been added for various hunting zones, including Isle of Prayer, Field of Whispers and others.
- New quests have been added, including one-time quests for the Neutral Zone, Ketra Orc Outpost, Varka Silenos Barracks and Sea of Spores hunting zones, as well as main quests and side quests for various locations.

## Homunculus Updates
- A new "heroic" Homunculus rank has been added, which can be obtained by evolving a top-grade Lv. 5 homunculus with 100% XP.
- Destroying any homunculus now yields evolution points, which can be used to evolve homunculi.

## Giran City Update
- The layout of Giran city has been modified and the appearance of Giran changes depending on the season.

## Einhasad's Store Update
- Einhasad's Store now sells a new item, Top-grade Homunculus' Hourglass and the purchase limit for the Dragon Skin Shirt has been changed.

## Crafting Menu Update
- The crafting menu limit no longer resets at 6:30 a.m. but instead when the crafting period starts.

## New Relic Collections
- new B-grade relic collections have been added.

## Item and Attribute Updates
- The text describes updates to the online game Lineage 2 Europe, including changes to various items and their attributes.
- New collections have been added, including "Elements of Our World," which provides a 3% increase to all critical rate and power.

## UI Updates
- The game's UI has been updated with new features, such as tooltips for items and improved item descriptions.

## Bug Fixes
- Various bugs and issues have been fixed, including problems with auto-compounding, teleportation and character stats.

## Item Deletion
- Some items, including the Amulet: Protection of Valakas and XP Bottle - 5000, will be deleted during a scheduled maintenance on April 17, 2024.
